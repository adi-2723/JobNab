Operations

Everything as simple as possible, but no simpler.

Employers can easily manage job listings and applications

Employers can create, edit, and delete job listings. They can specify job details such as title, description, requirements, and salary. This operation also allows employers to manage applications received for each listing

Job seekers can register and enhance their profiles

Job seekers can register on the platform, create profiles, and upload resumes. They can manage their profiles by updating personal information, work experience, and skills, making it easier for employers to find suitable candidates.

Job seekers can apply for jobs and track their status.

Job seekers can apply for job listings directly through the platform. They can track the status of their applications (e.g., submitted, under review, interviewed) and receive notifications about any updates from employers.
